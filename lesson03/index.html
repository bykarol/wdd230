Site Name

A Coruña Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Our purpose is the representation, promotion and defense of the general interests of commerce, industry, services and shipping, as well as the provision of services to companies that carry out the aforementioned activities.

Its fundamental objective is to ensure that companies in A Coruña become more competitive every day, helping them to adapt to the changes that are associated with new production models and an increasingly globalized and constantly changing market.
