Target Audience

Our target audience is people over 18 years of age, who have a small or medium-sized company domiciled in the City Council of A Coruña and want to contribute to local development.

Personas

Uxía Dans

Uxía owns a restaurant in Los Mallos district and she represents all the merchants of said place. Currently, the merchants of that area have been greatly affected by crime, they have filed complaints to the respective authorities and still have not received an answer. Uxía approaches to the A Coruña Chamber of Commerce seeking support for trade in the Mallos sector.

Iago Represas

Iago is a recent marketing graduate, he currently has a business where he wants to promote local commerce in the area where he lives. It is his desire to receive more knowledge about the steps to follow to achieve it, that is why he approaches the chamber of commerce to be aware of the different workshops and conferences that can help him in his goal.

Scenarios

  1. How can the Chamber of Commerce represent me before public entities to give greater weight to my requests?
  2. Will the Chamber of Commerce provide me with workshops and training on current regulations in my area and topics of interest?
  3. How can the chamber of commerce help me promote my business?
  4. How does the Chamber of Commerce defend the general interests of local businesses?
  5. Do I have to pay any fee to belong to the Chamber of Commerce of A Coruña?
